Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2026

This policy applies to paid subscription plans purchased by property owners on RentGoX to list additional properties or unlock extra features. It does not cover any rental transaction between an owner and a seeker conducted outside the app — RentGoX is not a party to those arrangements.

1. Subscription Plans

Owners may subscribe to a paid plan to increase the number of properties they can list or to access additional features. Plans are billed for the period stated at checkout (for example, 6 months or 1 year) and do not renew automatically — you choose when to purchase again.

2. Cancellation

You may stop using a plan at any time from within the app. Because plans are prepaid for a fixed term, cancelling a plan does not generate a refund for the unused period, except as described in the eligibility table below.

3. Refund Eligibility

SituationOutcome
Duplicate payment or payment deducted but plan not activatedFull refund
Technical error on our end preventing use of the purchased planFull or partial refund, reviewed case by case
Change of mind after a plan is active and functioning normallyNot eligible
Refund request made more than 7 days after purchaseNot eligible, except for unresolved technical issues

4. How to Request a Refund

Email us at supportrentgox@gmail.com with your registered phone number, the payment reference ID, and a short description of the issue. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

5. Processing Time

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. Your bank or payment provider may take additional time to reflect the refund in your statement.

6. Payment Processing

All payments on RentGoX are processed through a secure, PCI-compliant third-party payment gateway. RentGoX does not store your card, UPI, or bank account details on its own servers.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Refund & Cancellation Policy from time to time. Changes will become effective immediately after they are published on this page.
