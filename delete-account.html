Delete Your RentGoX Account

If you would like to permanently delete your RentGoX account and associated data, you can request account deletion by contacting us.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to supportrentgox@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My RentGoX Account.
  3. Include your registered mobile number or Google account email used with RentGoX.
  4. Our support team will verify your request and process it.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your RentGoX account.
  • Your profile information.
  • Your property listings.
  • Your uploaded property images.
  • Your saved preferences associated with your account.

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained for a limited period where required by applicable law, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or enforcement of our legal obligations.

Deletion Time

Most account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days.
